How to get to the local HEB:

- After coming out of the driveway, turn right and go to the first intersection
- If it is between the hours of 4pm and 7pm
  - Turn left and drive until you reach US 71
  - Turn right onto US 71
  - Turn right into the first entrance after the HEB sign
- Otherwise, turn right and follow the curved road until you reach William Cannon.
  - Turn right and drive down William Cannon until you reach US 71
  - Turn right onto US 71
  - Turn right into the first entrance after the HEB sign
- You've reached your destination
